# Research Gap

## Existing Research

Most insider threat detection studies focus on:

- Statistical anomaly detection
- Machine learning classifiers
- UEBA systems

Several commercial products exist:

- Microsoft Defender Insider Risk
- Splunk UBA
- Exabeam
- Securonix

## Limitations

### Gap 1

Most systems do not maintain a persistent digital representation of user behavior.

### Gap 2

Most studies focus solely on anomaly detection without integrating DLP context.

### Gap 3

Evidence preservation mechanisms are rarely considered.

### Gap 4

Investigation remains highly dependent on human analysts.

## Research Opportunity

A unified framework combining:

Digital Behavior Twin

UEBA

DLP

Blockchain Evidence Preservation

AI Investigation

may improve insider threat detection and forensic investigation.
