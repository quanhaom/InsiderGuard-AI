# Functional Requirements

## FR01

The system shall collect login events.

## FR02

The system shall collect file access events.

## FR03

The system shall collect USB device events.

## FR04

The system shall collect email activity events.

## FR05

The system shall create a Digital Behavior Twin for each user.

## FR06

The system shall calculate behavioral risk scores.

## FR07

The system shall detect abnormal activities.

## FR08

The system shall generate incidents.

## FR09

The system shall store incident evidence.

## FR10

The system shall calculate evidence hashes.

## FR11

The system shall record evidence hashes into blockchain ledger.

## FR12

The system shall verify evidence integrity.

## FR13

The system shall generate AI investigation reports.
